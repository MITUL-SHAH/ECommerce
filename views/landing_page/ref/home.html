What is ESMS?

ESMS is the short hand for Education Stock Market Simulator.

ESMS (pronounced as Es-mi-s) is a stock market simulator that aims to teach the fundamentals of stock investment for those who are willing to learn, especially to the crowd that's too timid to test the water in the real stock market.
